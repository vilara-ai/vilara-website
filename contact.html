Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is the free version really free?

Yes, completely free forever for companies under 5 employees that don't need all the features. No credit card required, no time limits. Includes core ERP modules and community support.

What happens when we grow past 5 employees or want the full 'blow my mind' features?

You can upgrade to the Professional plan anytime, just let us know when you're ready and we'll make sure you have a smooth transition to nirvana.

How long does a consultation take?

Consultations are typically 30 minutes and include a personalized demo, ROI analysis, and implementation roadmap tailored to your specific needs.

How quickly can we get started?

Small teams can start immediately with the free version. Larger companies can begin after consultation, typically within 1-2 weeks with full implementation support.

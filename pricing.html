Pricing FAQ

Common questions about Vilara pricing

Why $0 seat licenses?

We believe you should pay for value, not users. Adding employees shouldn't mean exponentially higher costs. Vilara pricing is based on company size and usage, not individual seats.

What happens when I grow?

Automatic upgrades based on employee count. No surprises - you'll be notified before any billing changes and can always review your usage in real-time.

Are there setup fees?

No setup fees for any plan. Implementation, data migration, training, and go-live support are all included in your subscription.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, no long-term contracts. You own your data and can export it anytime. We're confident you'll love Vilara, so we don't lock you in.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! Annual payment discounts, nonprofit pricing, and startup credits available. Contact sales for details.
